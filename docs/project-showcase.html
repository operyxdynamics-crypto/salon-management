Recommended Roadmap

This order keeps the salon operations panel usable before expanding marketplace and websites.

  1. Finish Calendar Operations Recurring bookings, blocked time, rooms/resources, drag resize, deeper edit flow, and mobile calendar polish.
  2. Finish POS Reversal Workflows Draft sales, voids, partial refunds, line returns, credit notes, and clearer reversal preview.
  3. Finalize CRM, Reports, and Settings Duplicate merge, imports/exports, report center, editable business policies, taxes, invoices, roles.
  4. Browser-harden Register / Day Closing Browser-test real open/close register, payment split, refunds, expenses, GST, tips, stock movements, variance, and CSV export.
  5. Then Build Marketplace and Salon Websites Only after salon daily operations are stable enough for real counter use.

Pilot Acceptance Checklist

The pilot salon should pass these workflows before real daily use.

  • Owner logs in and sees only authorized tenant and branch data.
  • Super Admin approves salon and branch publication.
  • Salon creates services, staff, products, customers, benefits, and stock.
  • Reception books appointment from dashboard or calendar.
  • Calendar shows marketplace, phone, walk-in, and staff-created bookings.
  • Appointment is checked in, completed, and checked out through POS.
  • GST or non-GST invoice is generated with correct numbering.
  • Offline cash, card, UPI, wallet, loyalty, gift card, and package payment records are correct.
  • Stock, commissions, loyalty, wallet, gift-card, and package balances update correctly.
  • Invoice opens, prints, and can be saved as PDF.
  • Register opens and closes with variance tracking.
  • Owner can review sales, GST, payments, inventory, expenses, staff earnings, and customer history.
Payments are offline records only. Messaging providers, online payments, custom domains, and statutory payroll are deferred milestones.
